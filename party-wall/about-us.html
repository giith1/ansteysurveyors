Our Expertise at Anstey Surveyors

At Anstey Surveyors, we provide bespoke solutions designed to address your unique requirements. Are you a building owner planning construction? A neighboring owner concerned about potential impacts? Whatever your situation, we’re here to help.

Our areas of expertise include:

  • Party Wall Surveys
    Conducting in-depth assessments to evaluate how proposed construction might affect neighboring properties, ensuring potential risks are identified and managed.

  • Preparation of Party Wall Notices
    Crafting and serving legally compliant notices to adjoining owners with professionalism and precision, meeting all legal obligations seamlessly.

  • Negotiation and Agreement of Party Wall Awards
    Acting as impartial surveyors or appointed representatives, we facilitate fair and equitable agreements, safeguarding the rights of all parties.

  • Expert Advice on Party Wall Matters
    Providing clear, dependable guidance on your rights and responsibilities under the Party Wall etc. Act 1996, empowering you to navigate complex regulations with ease.
